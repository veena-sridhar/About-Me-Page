Introduction

I just graduated with a degree in Chemistry. I can finally say that I understand why people keep saying that college was the best time in their lives. It was tons of fun! But once I started working, I realized that the biotech industry really didn't suit any of my interests. After that, I resolved to make a career change.

I'm extremely passionate about trying completely new things, and creating great art. Front-end development seems like the perfect hybrid between my science background and artistic inclinations. I'm really excited to see myself get better at this!

Where I'm From

I was born in Columbia, South Carolina, but when I was very young, my family moved to Silicon Valley, CA. Since I was raised in California, I grew up just as spoiled in terms of the weather as every other Californian out there. For college, I moved to sunny San Diego, CA, and ended up with a BS in Chemistry from UC San Diego.

Two weeks after graduation, I started working at Hologic, a biotech company in San Diego. Though the work hasn't quite piqued my interest, I keep myself fulfilled by learning new things outside of work.
